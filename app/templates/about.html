About


Folks Gab/Duel combines casual discussion with engaging debates.


Every post includes two authors. They are separated into two groups: Gab and Duel.

Gab

This part of the blog could focus on creating a platform for people to engage in open and constructive conversations. Topics include current events, pop culture, personal experiences, and more. Posts feature guest bloggers and interviews with individuals from various backgrounds, bringing diverse perspectives and insights to the community.

Duel

This section involves hosting debates or discussions where contributors express their opinions on various. Each author is on opposing sides of the topic or expresses dissimilar viewpoints. Duel also includes competitions or challenges, such as writing contests. These posts cover a wide range of topics, including anything from technology and science to arts and entertainment, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Intensity scale

Authors assign their posts an intensity level, which is indicated by the number of flames or stars. Flames are used for Duel posts, while stars are used for Gab posts. The scale ranges from 1 to 5, with 1 being the least intense and 5 being the most intense. Factors that affect intensity include the controversial nature of the topic, the author's tone, and the author's level of passion.
